# Favourite thing I wrote. ## Poem to my grandmother Hello, My name is Rathesa Kodeeswaran. The favourite thing that I wrote was a poem for my grandmother. It was my favourite because I got to express such beautiful words to her about how much our family appreciates and adores her. My entire family enjoyed my poetry, and I recall my family being together. I was grateful for all of the time we had together at the time. It makes me feel more grateful for the time we spend together.
